Artist Statement

My practice explores the relationship between material, surface, and hand-made expression. With a background in Leather Engineering, I approach painting and drawing through an understanding of texture, durability, treatment, and transformation of surfaces. My work bridges traditional art-making with material research and experimental finishes.
